Candy Mahjong

A Mahjong solitaire game with candy.

Mahjong is a Chinese game traditionally played with 14 tiles in 4 sets. The game is played by drawing and discarding tiles.

How to Play

Combine two of the same tiles to remove them from the playing field.

You can only use tiles that are:

  • Highlighted
  • Not covered by another tile
  • Has at least one side (left or right) open
