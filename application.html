Candy Majong

Overview

A majong soiltare game with candy. Majong is a Chines game played traditionaly played with 14 tiles in 4 sets played by drawing and disscarding cards.

How To Play

Combine two of the same tiles to remove tiles from playing feild.

You can only use tiles that are:

  • highlighted
  • Not covered by another
  • Has at least one side on the left or right open
