What is Downtown Oddities?

The Downtown Oddities store opened in Greeley, Colorado, in 2021. Offering a variety of antiques and novelty items. Featuring many one-of-a-kind vintage items. With an ever-changing selection, there are always new, strange collectables to be found. Fueling the strange and cryptic curiosities of their shoppers. At the Downtown Oddities shop, there is something for everyone.

We believe that shopping is an experience that stays with customers long after they have left the shop. We believe it is an experience that sticks with the customer long after they have left. Outside of just selling goods, we aim to create a memorable experience with our one-of-a-kind items.

We have a variety of items from unique vintage home decor to reclaimed bones. At the Downtown Oddities shop, there is something for everyone from the curious to the strange.
